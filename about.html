About 26 Triumphus

A software consulting and development firm built on the belief that great technology serves people first.

We turn complex problems into clear, working software.

26 Triumphus was founded on a simple conviction: the best software isn't just technically excellent — it solves real problems for real people. We bring together consulting rigor and engineering craft to help businesses build products they're proud of.

We work as a close extension of your team. That means honest conversations, shared ownership, and a commitment to long-term outcomes rather than short-term deliverables.

Whether you're a startup finding product-market fit or an established company navigating digital change, we adapt our approach to where you are and where you're going.

Our Values

What we stand for

These aren't platitudes — they're the principles that guide every engagement and every line of code.

Clarity over complexity

We resist unnecessary complexity in architecture, communication, and process. Simple, well-understood systems outlast clever ones.

Ownership without ego

We take full responsibility for our work and welcome feedback openly. The goal is always the best outcome for your business, not our pride.

Honest communication

We tell you what you need to hear, not just what you want to hear. No vague timelines, no hidden blockers, no pleasant surprises that mask real issues.

Long-term thinking

Every decision we make considers the future of your codebase and your organization. We build things that your team can maintain and evolve with confidence.
